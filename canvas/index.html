About

I have a B.S. in Computer Science from the University of San Diego, and I currently work as a Software Engineer in Test Automation at Illumina, a genetics company based in San Diego.

In my free time, I enjoy exploring San Diego, playing my ukulele and piano, cooking, and coding side projects. Please feel free to drop me a note, check out my blog, or just explore!
