NASA APOD is a simple iOS app that takes the NASA Astronomy Picture of the Day and displays it in app.

Endless Possibilities

Choose an APoD for any day between June 16th, 1995, when NASA started the program, and today.

Media can range from pictures of planets, telescopes, stars, comets, and so much more!

Spectacular Space

Get jaw-dropping images right to your phone on a daily basis. If NASA's blurb about the photo isn't enough for you, you can always tap on the image to go to NASA's website and learn more!
